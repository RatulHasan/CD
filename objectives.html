Objectives :

To make the credit and savings services available to the poor people and micro-entrepreneurs having potential to improve their social and economic condition.

To provide various development services enabling the poor and disadvantaged people to lead a better life.

To strengthen the capacity of the poor and disadvantaged rural households for sustainable development of their livelihood.

To introduce and implement new ideas and initiatives aiming to benefit the disadvantaged people of the society.

Core Values of CDIP :

  • Listening and respecting the people as valued clients
  • Responsiveness of client's needs as a key to the development
  • Ensuring access to flexible and quality financial services for the clients
  • Respecting client's right to deposit and withdraw savings, whenever necessary
  • Rules and regulations of CDIP are made to gear the needs and wishes of client's and stakeholders
  • Constant persuasion of innovative ideas to alleviate poverty and to improve socio-economic condition of people
  • Transparency, accountability and efficiency in all spheres and activities of CDIP are the most important element for good governance
